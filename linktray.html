Key Features

Instant System Tray Access

Your links live in your system tray, always available without opening your browser. One click shows your entire collection.

Smart Folder Organization

Create folders and subfolders to organize links by project, client, or any structure that makes sense for how you work.

One-Click Browser Capture

Press CTRL+L while browsing to instantly capture the current URL. No copying, pasting, or dialog boxes.

Intelligent Favorites

LinkTray automatically tracks your top 5 most-clicked links and keeps them in a Favorites folder for instant access.

Multiple Link Groups

Create separate collections for different projects or clients. Switch between groups instantly with a double-click.

Drag-and-Drop Management

Intuitive visual interface for organizing links. Move items anywhere with your mouse and see your structure at a glance.

How It Works

1

Browse to Any Web Page

Navigate to any website you want to save - project documentation, client dashboard, research paper, or tool.

2

Press CTRL+L

LinkTray instantly captures the URL and domain name from your active browser window. Works with Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and all modern browsers.

3

Organize with Drag-and-Drop

Open the Link Manager to arrange your links into folders, create groups for different projects, and customize your structure.

4

Access with One Click

Right-click the system tray icon, navigate your folder structure, and click any link to open it. Your Favorites are always at the top.
