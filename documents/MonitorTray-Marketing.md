## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will this work with my monitors?

If your monitors support DDC/CI (most modern monitors do), then yes! MonitorTray will detect them automatically.

### Do I need administrator rights?

No! MonitorTray runs with standard user permissions.

### Can I control monitors connected to different computers?

MonitorTray controls monitors connected to the PC it's running on. Each computer needs its own instance.

### What if my monitor doesn't support brightness control?

MonitorTray will show which features each monitor supports. If your monitor doesn't support a feature, it simply won't be available for that display.

### Does it support USB-connected monitors?

DDC/CI typically works over HDMI, DisplayPort, and DVI. USB monitors may not support the protocol.

### Will scheduled turn-offs work if my computer is asleep?

No, your computer must be running for scheduled turn-offs to execute. However, you can use Windows Task Scheduler to wake your PC before scheduled times if desired.

### Can I turn off just some monitors?

Absolutely! You have individual control over each connected monitor.

### Does it remember my settings?

Yes! All your friendly names, display order, brightness preferences, and schedules are saved automatically.
